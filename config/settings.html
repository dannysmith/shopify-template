Opening Times

You can write whatever you want in the Opening Times (text) column and it will appear on your website unchanged.

The Opening Times (shhema.org) column is used to expose your opening times to goole. They must follow a specific format:

  • Times are specified using 24:00 time. For example, 3pm is specified as 15:00.
  • A time range is seperated with a dash.
  • For example: 9am to 5pm would be written 09:00-17:00.

More information is available on the schema.org page.
